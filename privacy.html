Privacy Policy

Merlin — a private personal AI assistant service ("Merlin," "the Service")
Operated by Dustin Mooney ("the Operator") · Last updated: July 10, 2026

1. Scope

This policy describes how the Service handles information for its users. Merlin is a private, invite-only personal assistant operated for the Operator and a small number of individuals he personally invites ("Users"). It is not offered to the public, has no public registration, and is not a commercial product.

2. Information the Service handles

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, birthday, and per-user preferences (assistant name, personality settings, voice selection) provided at account creation.
  • Conversation content: messages exchanged with the assistant across its channels (web, messaging apps, SMS/RCS, voice calls), including extracted "memory" facts the assistant retains to serve the User.
  • Connected-service data: where a User explicitly connects an account (for example a calendar or email account), the Service accesses that data solely to fulfill the User's own requests. Access tokens are encrypted at rest.
  • Usage records: an internal ledger of feature and model usage (timestamps, token counts, computed costs) kept for cost accounting and abuse prevention.

3. How information is used

Information is used for exactly one purpose: providing the assistant's functionality to the User it belongs to. The Service does not sell, rent, or share personal information; it serves no advertising; it performs no cross-user profiling. Each User's data is isolated from every other User's at the database layer.

4. Processing by AI providers

To generate responses, the content of a User's request (and relevant context) is transmitted to third-party AI model providers (such as Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, and others) under their respective API terms. Voice audio is processed by a speech provider to enable spoken conversations. Text-message delivery is handled by a telecommunications provider. These providers act as processors for the Service; the Service does not permit them to use its data for advertising.

5. Messaging (SMS/RCS)

The Service sends text messages only in reply to messages initiated by a registered, allowlisted User. It sends no marketing, no bulk messages, and no messages to third parties. Message frequency varies with the User's own use. Message and data rates may apply. Text STOP to stop all messages; text HELP for assistance.

6. Storage, security, and retention

  • Data is stored on infrastructure controlled by the Operator, protected by passkey (WebAuthn) authentication, encrypted transport (TLS), database-level per-user isolation, and encrypted secrets.
  • Every action the assistant takes on a User's behalf is recorded in an audit log.
  • Backups are taken automatically and retained on a rolling basis.
  • Conversation history and memories are retained until the User asks for their removal or the account is closed, at which point associated data is deleted from the live system.

7. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and no accounts are created for them.

8. Your choices

Users may review their stored memories in the application, request correction or deletion of their data, disconnect any connected service at any time, and stop text messaging with STOP. Because the Service is private and invite-only, requests are handled directly by the Operator.

9. Changes

Material changes to this policy will be reflected on this page with an updated date above.
