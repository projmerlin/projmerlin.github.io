Privacy Policy

Merlin — a private personal AI assistant. Effective July 2026.

What Merlin is

Merlin is a private, invite-only personal assistant service operated by Dustin Mooney for himself and invited members of his household. It is not a public service and has no public signup.

Messaging

Merlin sends text messages (SMS/RCS) only in reply to messages initiated by the account owner from a registered, allowlisted phone number. It never sends unsolicited messages, marketing, or messages to third parties. Reply STOP at any time to stop all messages; reply HELP for help.

Data

Conversations are stored privately on infrastructure operated by the owner, isolated per user, and used solely to provide the assistant's functionality to that user. Data is never sold, shared, or used for advertising.

Transparency

Merlin is an AI assistant and always identifies itself as one.
